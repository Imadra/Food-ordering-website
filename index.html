WHAT MADE US GREAT IS STILL WHAT MAKES US GREAT

AR Corporation, based in Astana, is one of the few brands in Kazakhstan that can boast a rich, decades-long history of success and innovation. It all started with two cooks who created a soon-to-be world-famous recipe more than 15 years ago, a list of secret herbs and spices scratched out on the back of the door to their kitchen. Those cooks were Rassul and Abylay, of course, and now AR is the Kazakhstan’s most popular fast-food restaurant chain, specializing in that same Original Recipe®, sandwiches and pizzas.
